# Number

A simple game in Lua. The goal is to guess a number.

## How to build and run

1. Download the _main.lua_ to your computer.
2. Run it with `lua main.lua`.

## How to play

```txt
Guess my number between 1 and 100!

Your guess: 50
Too low!
Your guess: 75
Too high!
Your guess: 62
Too high!
Your guess: 56
Too high!
Your guess: 53
Too low!
Your guess: 54
Too low!
Your guess: 55

You win, the number was 55!
You had 7 attempts.
```
